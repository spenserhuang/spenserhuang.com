bio

I love building beautiful and meaningful things, contemplating about the “why”, and experiencing different lifestyles. My goal this year is to better understand myself. Thus, I will be going on many adventures as a means of personal discovery.


I get bored easily so I'm constantly working on interesting projects. Currently I am working on a private growth community, a startup that helps people unwind and grow via travel, a community for Taiwanese Americans, and a super secret private bar project!


In addition to my passion for entrepreneurship, I am also an avid bartender and adventurer who is constantly searching for the next big journey.
