bio

I love building beautiful and meaningful things, contemplating about the “why”, and experiencing different lifestyles. My goal this year is to better understand myself. Thus, I will be going on different adventures and trying out all sorts of projects.


I'm constantly working on interesting projects. As of late, I have been super interested in blockchain technology and have been spending a lot of time researching and blogging about it. Additionally. I am working on a private growth community, a blockchain club that educates Northwestern students about the technology, a community for Taiwanese Americans, and a super secret private cocktail bar!


In addition to my passion for entrepreneurship, I am also an avid bartender and adventurer who is constantly searching for the next big journey.
